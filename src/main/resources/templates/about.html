About Hoops Club

Our journey, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Hoops Club began as a small community basketball program with just 20 players. What started as a passion project by our founder, Coach Michael Reynolds, has grown into one of the most respected basketball organizations in the region.

Today, we serve over 500 players annually across our various programs, from youth development to competitive adult leagues. Our state-of-the-art facilities and professional coaching staff provide an unparalleled basketball experience.

We take pride in our inclusive environment that welcomes players of all skill levels and backgrounds, fostering both athletic excellence and personal growth.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day

Mission

To develop skilled basketball players and outstanding individuals through quality coaching, competitive opportunities, and a supportive community that values teamwork, respect, and continuous improvement.

Core Values

  • Excellence in coaching and player development
  • Sportsmanship and respect for all
  • Community engagement and outreach
  • Inclusivity and accessibility
  • Lifelong love of the game

Vision

To be recognized as the premier basketball development program in our region, known for producing not only talented players but also individuals who exemplify leadership, character, and a commitment to their communities.

Our History

Key milestones in our journey

1995

Hoops Club founded by Coach Michael Reynolds with one youth team of 20 players

2002

Opened our first dedicated basketball facility with two full courts

2008

Launched our competitive travel team program

2012

Expanded to include women's and girls' programs

2016

Moved to our current 5-court facility with fitness center

2020

Celebrated our 25th anniversary with over 500 active players
